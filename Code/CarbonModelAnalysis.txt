Model derivation
for Rewiring the carbon cycle : a theoretical framework for animal - driven ecosystem carbon sequestration
by Matteo Rizzuto, Shawn J. Leroux, and Oswald J. Schmitz
In this notebook, we describe development of our model accounting for the influence of animals on ecosystem carbon cycling . The manuscript contains a description of the model's mathematical structure and analyses rationale. Here, for each modeling scenario described in the manuscript, we:
- solve for equilibrium,
- evaluate the feasibility conditions of any biologically meaningful equilibrium\[LongDash]i.e., equilibria where all state variables are >0, 
- perform a local stability analyses, and
- derive expressions to estimate values of plant biomass accumulation, primary productivity, and net ecosystem carbon balance at equilibrium
